Mindfulness Club Cleveland gathers people of all backgrounds to practice presence, cultivate stillness, and support one another on the path of mindful living.

⁂

Everyone is welcome here.

Whether you've been practicing for decades or have never tried meditation before, our community holds a seat for you. We practice together in the spirit of openness, kindness, and honest inquiry.

What we offer

🧘

Weekly Sits

Regular silent meditation gatherings, open to all levels, every Tuesday evening.

🌲

Outdoor Practice

Mindful gatherings and nature-based sessions in Cleveland's beautiful parks and lakefront.

🤝

Community

A genuine sangha — a community of practice, mutual support, and shared reflection.
