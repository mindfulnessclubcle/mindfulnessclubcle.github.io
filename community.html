In Our Community

Connection, practice, and shared experience

A breathing, living community

While our weekly sits are the foundation, much of our community lives in the connections that form around them. Practice doesn’t end when the bell rings — it continues in conversation, creativity, and everyday life.

Members of our community regularly host their own gatherings — from meditation sits to creative sessions to simple moments of connection. These offerings are informal, evolving, and open.

How we show up

There is no single way to participate. Some come to sit quietly. Others come to connect, reflect, or create. All are welcome.

What matters most is presence — showing up as you are, and allowing others to do the same.

Ways to Engage

  • Join a weekly sit or discussion
  • Attend a member-hosted gathering
  • Connect one-on-one with others

Start an Offering

  • Have an event you want to share?
  • Want to host a meditation or creative session?
  • Reach out — we’ll help you share it
