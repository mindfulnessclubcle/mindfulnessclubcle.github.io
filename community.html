In Our Community

Exploring connection, presence, and shared growth in Cleveland.

What This Means

Our community is rooted in intentional presence and genuine connection. We believe mindfulness is not just an individual practice, but something that grows through shared experience.

Whether through conversation, silence, or creative expression, we aim to cultivate a space where people can show up as they are and feel supported.

How We Gather

  • Weekly mindfulness sits and reflections
  • Open discussions on awareness, purpose, and growth
  • Creative practices like music, journaling, and art
  • Community-based service and outreach

Events Hosted by Our Members

These are ongoing offerings created and led by members of our community.

Sunday Morning Sit

When: Sundays, 10:00 AM

Where: Edgewater Park

A quiet group meditation followed by optional reflection and conversation. Open to all experience levels.

Midweek Reset

When: Wednesdays, 7:00 PM

Where: Tremont Area

A guided mindfulness session to slow down, reconnect, and reset during the week.

Monthly Creative Night

When: First Friday of each month

Where: Rotating locations

Music, journaling, art, and shared expression in a relaxed, supportive space.

Values We Practice

  • Presence: Meeting each moment with awareness
  • Openness: Welcoming diverse perspectives and experiences
  • Care: Supporting one another with intention
  • Curiosity: Exploring inner and outer worlds

Get Involved
